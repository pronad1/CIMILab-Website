Posenjit

Co-Founder

Co-Founder, CIMILab

MU Institute for Data Science and Informatics · University of Missouri

Posenjit is the co-founder of the Computation Informatics and Machine Intelligence Lab (CIMILab). With a deep commitment to advancing AI research at the intersection of computational methods and real-world applications, Posenjit helped establish CIMILab's research vision and foundational infrastructure. The lab operates at the University of Missouri under the MU Institute for Data Science and Informatics (MUIDSI), pursuing trustworthy and reproducible AI for high-stakes applications in medicine and beyond.
