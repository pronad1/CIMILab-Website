CIMILab is growing! We are delighted to welcome two new members joining the lab at the start of the new academic year:

- **Aisha Rahman** joins as a **PhD student**, working on interpretable deep learning for stroke lesion segmentation as part of the DSANet-ISLES project. Aisha brings a strong background in medical image analysis and machine learning.

- **Priya Nair** joins as a **Research Assistant**, contributing to our CIMILab-Bench and CIMILab-DataOps infrastructure projects. Priya's engineering background will be invaluable in building our reproducibility tooling.

Welcome to the lab, Aisha and Priya!
