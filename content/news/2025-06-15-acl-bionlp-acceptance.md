Great news from CIMILab! Our paper **"Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Clinical Decision Support: Reducing Hallucinations in Biomedical LLMs"** by Carlos Mendez and Dr. Tanmoy Dey has been accepted at the **ACL BioNLP Workshop 2025**.

The paper demonstrates that RAG pipelines grounded in clinical guidelines reduce factual hallucination by 42% compared to vanilla fine-tuning. Full paper and code will be released upon camera-ready submission.

Congratulations to Carlos on this first publication!
