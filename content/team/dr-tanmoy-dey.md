Dr. Dey is the founder and director of the Computation Informatics and Machine Intelligence Lab (CIMILab). His research focuses on building trustworthy, interpretable AI systems for high-stakes clinical and scientific applications — particularly medical imaging AI for stroke and spinal lesion analysis. Before founding CIMILab, he developed expertise in deep learning architectures, ensemble methods, and evaluation frameworks for biomedical AI. He is committed to reproducible, open science and to mentoring the next generation of AI researchers.
